Mayurbhanj, Baripada

Explore Mayurbhanj, Baripada

Mayurbhanj is a culturally rich district in Odisha, known for its unique traditions, beautiful landscapes, and historical landmarks. Below are some notable places to explore:

Maa Kichakeswari Temple

Located in Khiching, this ancient temple is dedicated to Goddess Kichakeswari and is known for its intricate stone carvings and spiritual significance.

Mayurbhanj Chhau Dance

Mayurbhanj is renowned for its Chhau dance, a UNESCO-recognized art form that combines martial arts, tribal traditions, and storytelling.

Mayurbhanj District

A map showcasing the district's geography, surrounded by lush forests and dotted with cultural landmarks.

Seal of Odisha

Odisha's state seal reflects the region's governance and cultural heritage, symbolizing unity and pride.

Similipal National Park

A UNESCO Biosphere Reserve, Similipal is home to diverse flora and fauna, offering a breathtaking experience for nature enthusiasts.

Baripada Town

The cultural hub of Mayurbhanj, Baripada is famous for its Rath Yatra and serves as the district's headquarters.
